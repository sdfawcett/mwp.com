Our Process

Every one of our Shopify build projects has the same four milestones. Here's what you can expect when you work with Mindful Web Partnership:

Project Milestone 1
During the kickoff call we'll go over the project structure and important documents. We'll also talk about your new store's technical specifications.
Project Milestone 2
For the second project milestone we take a tour of the new store's admin dashboard. And we cover any additional apps or functionality.
Project Milestone 3
The third project milestone is a tour of the new store's front end shopping experience. We'll also do some simple theme edit training.
Project Milestone 4
The last project milestone is launch, where we'll hand over the keys to your new Shopify store. We'll also resolve open questions, carry out a test order, and talk about any next steps.

FAQ: We have answers.

  • What type of projects do you take on?

    We do eCommerce projects primarily on the Shopify platform — things like theme customizations, store setups, platform migrations, store and sales-funnel optimizations, Shopify 2.0 upgrades, app integrations, and various types of training.

  • What’s so good about Shopify?

    COVID-19 is pushing many more businesses online, and we think the Shopify platform has the most momentum and long-term potential. It’s an accessible, robust eCommerce platform that offers a lot of features out-of-the-box. It removes so much complexity for end-users, while offering powerful features to developers who build Shopify solutions.

    Now that Google Shopping is funneling more users to Shopify stores than ever before, there couldn't be a better time to invest in a new Shopify store.

  • What is Shopify 2.0?

    Shopify is already a top-notch platform, and they’re making it even better with Shopify 2.0. The most exciting changes for clients are all the new ways to customize your online store (with or without code). Things like sections on every page, app blocks, support for dynamic content sources, and collection filtering open up a lot of new possibilities for online store owners.

    Also the new theme architecture means it’s easier for Mindful to make your Shopify store blazing fast so you get the best possible performance.

  • Shopify makes eCommerce seem easy. Can’t I just build and maintain a store myself?

    Some clients just want someone to do their store setup so they can take it and run with it — we're happy to do that. Down the road, if you get stuck and need help we can do that too.

    For anyone interested in building a more complex eCommerce solution, the more important question is “do you have the time and patience to learn how to build and maintain it”? If you don’t have that kind of time to spend, and you want to get the most out of your store from day one, Mindful can help.

  • How do you charge for projects?

    We provide flat-rate quotes for our store design and development packages, so you'll know exactly what your investment will be from the start.

  • I already have a Shopify store. Can Mindful help me make it better?

    We can definitely help you with your existing Shopify store. We'll consult with you to determine your goals and preferences, create design mockups or prototypes if necessary, and execute your vision for your store.
